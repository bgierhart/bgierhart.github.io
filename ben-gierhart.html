About Ben Gierhart

Ben Gierhart is an actor, writer, playwright and director who is based in Louisville, KY. He is a founding member of the Derby City Playwrights, a collective dedicated to creating new and exciting plays in Louisville. His play "Another Man's Treasure" was the winner of the 2016 SETC Charles M. Getchell New Play Award, and his play "The Moon in the Water" is currently in development with Pandora Productions, Louisville's LGBTQ theatre company. Arts and other journalistic writing of his has appeared in Arts-Louisville, The Voice Tribune, Modern Louisville and more. Feel free to say hi to him!
